About

Gold Rock Beach is part of The Lucayan National Park and is protected by the Bahamas National Trust.
At low tide, the shoreline of the beach spans for yards into the sea, and it is referred to as Grand Bahama Island's "welcome mat."
The turquoise water is exceptionally clear, calm, and shallow, making it a perfect play place for families with young children.
Adventurous travelers will revel in the offerings of Gold Rock Beach Bahamas. Across the road from this beach is the Lucayan Caverns, one of the world’s longest underground cave systems.
Best Time to Visit: June to Decembern
