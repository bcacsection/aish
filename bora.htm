About

Matira beach counts as one of the best beaches in the entire world. No doubt, this pristine shore with powdered sand kissing the waters with multiple shades of blue owes a mandatory visit.
The white sand slopes gently into a sandy bottomed lagoon so the water is warm and shallow. The floor is flat and has a sandy, coral-free bottom to walk on.
The peaceful island nightlife means that after-hours activity is limited to romantic strolls on the beach and quiet drinks at intimate beach bars - an ideal getaway for some travelers.
The beach wraps around a peninsula, and it's a popular spot for watching the sunset, snorkeling, and topless sunbathing.
Best Time to Visit: June to Decembern
