About

Canarian artist Cesar Manrique is the talent behind the beauty of Playa Jardin, a picturesque beach in Puerto de la Cruz
His vision of creating an attractive coastal landscape really comes alive here; palm trees and long expanses of volcanic sand surrounded by colourful botanical gardens result in a charming and elegant resort.
A waterfall cascades over volcanic rocks and little pools filled with marine life can be found dotted around. The soft, dark-coloured sand rolls into the warm waters with one calm area being used by swimmers while surfers enjoy the waves elsewhere in the ocean.
Sit back, relax and take in the views of Tenerife s volcano Mount Teide.
Best Time to Visit: During winter season
